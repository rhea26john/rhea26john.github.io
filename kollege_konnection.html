About Kollege Konnection

Kollege Konnection is a college classified website that allows college students to post free ads. It provides a place that makes it easy for college students to connect with other college students from their campus. Students can buy, sell, or trade textbooks, post ads about student housing, jobs & internships, items lost or found, events, services wanted or offered, items for sale, find new friends, etc.
